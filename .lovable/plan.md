
# MI Enterprises - Manufacturing Company Website

## Overview
A modern, professional single-page business website for MI Enterprises, an established (1984) manufacturer of rust-proof iron doors and window frames. Industrial design with black, grey, and orange color theme.

## Color Theme
- **Primary**: Orange (industrial accent)
- **Background**: Dark charcoal/black with grey sections
- **Text**: White on dark, dark on light sections
- **Accents**: Orange for CTAs, highlights, and borders

## Sections

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Fixed top nav with company name/logo, section links, and "Get a Custom Order" CTA button
- Mobile hamburger menu

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-width dark background with industrial texture feel
- Bold headline: "Strong, Rust-Proof Iron Doors & Frames Since 1984"
- Subtext about durability, CNC precision, quality
- Orange "Get a Custom Order" CTA button
- Badge showing "Est. 1984" and "40+ Years of Excellence"

### 3. About Us
- Company history since 1984
- Key stats: 40+ years, CNC precision, rust-proof specialization
- Clean two-column layout with text and feature highlights

### 4. Products
- Three product cards: Iron Sheet Doors, Window Frames, Custom Fabrication
- Each card with placeholder industrial image, description, and key features
- Highlights: durable, rust-resistant, precision manufactured, imported iron sheets

### 5. Why Choose Us
- Six feature cards in a grid:
  - 40+ Years Experience
  - Rust Proof GP Base Material
  - Imported Iron Sheets
  - CNC Machine Precision
  - Custom Orders Available
  - Strong & Durable Products
- Icons with orange accents

### 6. Manufacturing Process
- Horizontal timeline/steps: Design → CNC Cutting → Fabrication → Finishing → Delivery
- Clean visual flow with numbered steps

### 7. Gallery
- Grid of placeholder images showing doors, frames, CNC machines, workshop
- Lightbox-style hover effects

### 8. Contact Section
- Two-column layout: contact info on left, form on right
- Contact details: address, phone, email
- Form fields: Name, Phone, Message, Order Requirement
- Orange submit button

### 9. Footer
- Company info, quick links, contact details
- Copyright notice

## Design & UX
- Smooth scroll animations on section entry
- Mobile-responsive throughout
- Strong industrial typography
- Professional, trustworthy feel suitable for B2B manufacturing
